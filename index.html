Guidelines

PEACE - Thắp Sáng Bình Yên Trong Tim Bạn - Light Up The Peace In Your Heart

The first time you burn, you DO NOT cut the candle wick to ensure the maximum amount of wax is burned as possible.

In case the candle wick is dusty, it needs to be cut off. It is recommended to keep the candle length 3-5 mm to help maintain a steady flame, and the candle wax to melt evenly.

It is recommended to use a lighter with a long nozzle to help burn the candle safely and extinguish the candle with a special tool.
